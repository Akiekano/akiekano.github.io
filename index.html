Characters

Tohka Yatogami (夜刀神 十香)

Tohka is a girl with very long, dark-purple hair usually tied back with a ribbon of sorts, and matching dark purple eyes with white irises.
she had a cold, and emotionless demeanor. She was very wary of humans due to her being constantly attacked by the AST before Shido finally got the chance to talk to her.

Sheele (シェーレ)

Sheele was a slender woman with long purple hair and purple eyes.
Sheele was an airhead, often losing her glasses or failing at any chores she tried to do due to clumsiness, however, she was very kind and caring to her friends, willing to sacrifice her life for them, as seen with Mine..

Echidna (エキドナ)

was the Witch of Greed. Mentioned throughout the series
Echidna's curiosity toward the unknown and her thirst for knowledge are the two things that define her behavior, At first glance, she is polite, soft-spoken, understanding, and even a bit childish. She is stated by the other Witches to be quite clumsy like the time when she accidentally set the forest on fire, nearly burning to death, as she was trying to warm herself..
